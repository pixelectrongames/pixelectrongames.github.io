PRIVACY POLICY

Updated at March 15th, 2026

Thank you for playing our Games!
This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in connection with our games.

By downloading or using our Game, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Overview of Data Handling

Our game:

  • Does not require account registration with us
  • Does not directly collect personal data such as name, email, or phone number
  • Does not operate its own external servers to store user personal information
  • Relies on trusted third-party services (Google Play Services, Google AdMob, and Firebase Realtime Database) for certain features

Some data may be processed automatically by third-party providers as described below.

Information We Do Not Directly Collect

We do not directly collect or store:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Government ID numbers
  • Payment card details
  • Personal photos or media
  • Contact lists

Our games can be played without submitting personal information to us.

Gameplay Data and Local Storage

Our Game stores gameplay-related data locally on your device and optionally in cloud storage (Firebase). This may include:

  • Game progress
  • Level completion status
  • Achievements progress
  • In-game currency (such as coins or gems)
  • Settings and preferences

If you uninstall the game, locally stored data may be permanently deleted. We do not access or transfer your local gameplay data to our own servers outside Firebase.

Google Play Games Services

Our Game integrates Google Play Games Services for features such as: Player sign-in, Achievements, and Leaderboards.

When you choose to sign in using Google Play Games:

  • Google may process your Google account information
  • Your Player ID, profile name, and profile image may be accessible within our Game
  • Google may store and synchronize game progress data if cloud save is used

All such data handling is governed by Google’s own Privacy Policy. We do not control how Google collects, processes, or stores this data. For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Our Game may offer optional in-app purchases, including virtual items such as Gems or ad removal:

  • Certain items are consumable and can be purchased multiple times
  • Non-consumable items are purchased once
  • All transactions are processed via the Google Play Store billing system
  • We do not collect, store, or process your payment information
  • Refunds are handled according to Google Play Store policies

Advertising

The Game may display advertisements via third-party providers such as Google AdMob.

  • Advertisers may collect technical information such as device type, IP address, or app usage data
  • Ads may be personalized depending on region and consent

We are not responsible for the content, practices, or privacy policies of any third-party advertisers.

Analytics

Our Games do not use independent third-party analytics services such as Firebase Analytics or Google Analytics for behavioral tracking. Limited technical information may still be processed automatically by Google Play Services or AdMob for functionality, security, or ad delivery purposes.

Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Notice)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), data processed by third-party services may be handled under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • User consent
  • Legitimate interest (such as service functionality and fraud prevention)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

CCPA Notice (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you may have rights regarding personal information collected by third parties through our Game. Our Game does not sell personal information. However, advertising partners may process data for advertising purposes under their own policies.

Data Security & Retention

We rely on trusted third-party platforms (Google Play Services, Firebase, and AdMob) to maintain data security. Gameplay data stored locally remains on your device unless you delete the Game, clear app data, or reset your device. Cloud-synced data may remain in Firebase until deleted or overwritten by future saves.
